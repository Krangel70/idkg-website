About IDKG

IDKG Limited provides independent print consultancy and procurement services, helping businesses source, manage and deliver high-quality print projects across the UK and internationally.

Founded by Ian Krangel, IDKG works with a trusted network of specialist suppliers to ensure every project is matched with the right production partner, balancing quality, cost, turnaround time and service.
